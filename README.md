# DAT-300 Auto Part Sales Data

**_Summary_**
 This project focused on a dataset provided by an auto part manufacturer that detailed recorded orders of parts. The stakeholders were wanting to gather information to help determine what aspects should be of note or of a higher concern. Key questions that were asked by the stakeholder were the following:
 _1. Which deal sizes yield the largest volumes?_
 _2. Which territory generates the largest amount of sales?_
 _3. In which months do we see the largest volume of sales?_

**_Results & Analysis_**
  For this dataset, a Power BI dashboard was created to help create visuals that were asked by the client and visuals that would help better understand the entire dataset. These visuals included a total deal size amount and sale table, a vertical column chart for total deal size amount, a horizontal row chart displaying order amount by month, and an additional donut chart that shows order quantity by product line. In regards to the stakeholder questions, Medium sized orders yielded the largest volume of all recorded orders while November was the month with the largest volume of sales. The client's second question could not be answered due to there being no column within the dataset that recorded where order sales occured.
